About Us

AnimeWar is an anime Quote publishing website focused on the Japanese and Asian entertainment industry on a global scale. We are a subsidiary, or sister site, of Monsters and Critics.

If You Click The Refresh Button The New Anime Quote Will Render

We bring you the latest Quotes about anime, manga, manhwa, light novels. Launched in july 2023. Our focus is on predicting what will come next in the anime world based on our insider sources and insight into the industry. Our goal is to bring anime fans what they want while enjoying our work.
